Social Media Presence

Social media and social networking sites present tremendous opportunities for all kinds of business. In the digital era, almost everyone is using social media for their personal life's, networking and work.

Identify Your Goals and Priorities

Social media can have a significant impact on your business, but you need to have some direction or else you will be wasting your time. For example, one of the goals can be to find new clients, networking with other professionals and increasing traffic to their website or blog. Social media can be used to improve name recognition and branding, finding a new job and enjoying the activity with the community.

Focus on Quality Over Quantity

When it comes to social media there is a huge difference in high-quality connections and low-quality connections. Having quality interaction with others and making an impact is possible even without having a lot of followers, fans, or friends. Target a Few Specific Social Media Sites

There are literally hundreds of social media sites out there, and there is no way anyone can or should use them all. For example, it is good to start out with Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and LinkedIn as those are the major social media networks available for us. When choosing your targeted sites, keep your goals and priorities in mind. LinkedIn is perfect for landing that dream job. Twitter, Facebook and Instagram is good for networking, traffic audience and so on. For designers, there are many social networks such as Dribble and counting.
